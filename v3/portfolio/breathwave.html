Breathwave

A three-year partnership with a Vancouver breathwork practice. Built in 2023, re-optimized in 2026.

  • Role Web development
  • Period Dec 2023 to present
  • Site breathwave.net →

An online home for Breathwave that runs on sustainable energy. After the original 2023 launch, the site attracted as many unique visitors in one month as the previous Wix site had drawn in a full year, with near-perfect Lighthouse scores (100/100 SEO, 100/100 Best Practices, 98/100 Accessibility) on day one.

In April 2026 we returned and re-optimized for speed and accessibility. The visual design stayed intact, but everything underneath got faster and friendlier: the site now loads in roughly one second on mobile, down from close to twenty.

Since launching our revamped site by Gabriel, we've attracted as many unique visitors in one month as we did in an entire year with our old site. Nicole Stratton, Breathwave Team
